South Korea

*In a country where summer is very hot, it’s surprising to hear that people are scared of electric fans. Known as Fan Death, there is an urban legend that leaving a fan pointing at you will lead to certain death.*In city centres around Korea you can find colourful hotels without windows that are a bit too bright and colourful to be normal hotels. These are love motels, discrete hotels with sometimes extravagantly decorated rooms and a focus on anonymity. As the name suggests, these are places where couples can meet up for… love.*Korea is a land of tall apartment blocks and skyscrapers, so it sounds weird that there would be no 4th floor, right? Well, the 4th floor has been replaced with the ‘F floor’ instead (F for four, I assume). The reason for this is due to the pronunciation for the Chinese character for death, which is the same as the number 4. To avoid wishing bad luck on a building, or the people riding the elevator, the number 4 was removed and replaced with F*Almost 99% people in South Korea have the surename a"Kim".
